Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is EasyBus?

A: EasyBus is an online platform for booking bus tickets, providing a seamless experience for travelers.

Q: How do I book a ticket?

A: You can book a ticket by selecting your route, choosing a bus, and completing the payment process on our website.

Q: Can I cancel my booking?

A: Yes, you can cancel your booking through your account dashboard, but please check the cancellation policy for details.

Booking Policy

1. Booking Confirmation: - All bookings made through EasyBook are confirmed via email or SMS, containing a booking reference number and ticket details. It is essential to provide a valid email address or phone number during booking to receive confirmation and updates.


2. Payment Policy: - Full payment is required at the time of booking. EasyBook accepts various payment methods including credit/debit cards and online payment gateways. All transactions are processed securely to ensure the protection of your financial information.

3. Cancellation and Refunds: - Cancellations made within 24 hours of booking are eligible for a full refund. Cancellations made after this period may incur a fee, and the amount refunded will be based on the fare rules and timing of the cancellation. Please refer to our detailed cancellation policy for specific conditions and procedures.

4. Changes and Modifications: - Modifications to bookings, including changes in travel dates, times, or passenger details, can be made up to 24 hours before the scheduled departure time. Changes are subject to availability and may incur additional charges. Contact our customer support for assistance with modifications.

5. No-Show Policy: - In the event of a no-show, where the passenger does not board the bus as scheduled, the booking will be considered void and no refunds or rebookings will be provided. It is important to arrive at the departure point at least 15 minutes before the scheduled departure time to avoid missing your bus.

These policies ensure a clear understanding between EasyBook and our customers, facilitating a smooth booking experience and helping to manage expectations.
